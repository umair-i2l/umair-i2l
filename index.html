Privacy Policy for Weddi:

When you use Weddi, we may collect certain information from you, such as your name, age, location, and preferences. This information is necessary to provide you with personalized matching and proximity features.

We use the collected data to facilitate connections between users based on their preferences and proximity. Your information will only be shared with other users when there is a mutual match. Rest assured, we do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security:

We employ robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. We utilize encryption techniques and follow industry best practices to ensure the confidentiality and integrity of your information.

User Consent:

By using Weddi, you consent to the collection and use of your personal information as outlined in this Privacy Policy. You have the right to modify or delete your data at any time through the app's settings.

Age Restrictions:

Weddi is intended for users who are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If we become aware that a user is under 18, we will promptly delete their information.

Updates to the Privacy Policy:

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
